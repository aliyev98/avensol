Privacy Policy Your Privacy, Our Priority

This policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy governs how Avensol LLC handles and protects personal data in accordance with global data privacy standards such as the GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable regulations. Whether you are a prospective partner, a visitor to our site, or an existing customer, we are fully dedicated to handling your information responsibly. From the moment you land on our website to every email subscription or contact form you submit, we treat your data with utmost care and use it only for the reasons we clearly state. This policy applies to all digital touchpoints — including our corporate website, email communications, newsletters, social media interactions, and more.

2. What We Collect and How
2.1. Information You Provide Directly
When you voluntarily submit information through forms, newsletters, surveys, or inquiries, we collect:
  • Your name, company name, job title
  • Contact information (email, phone, address)
  • Your preferences or interests in our services
  • Any messages or content you submit via our platform
  • Authorities or regulators when legally required
This information helps us respond appropriately, personalize your experience, and build strong business relationships.

2.2. Automatically Collected Data
We use digital tools such as cookies, tags, and analytics scripts to automatically gather information like:
  • Your name, company name, job title
  • Browser type and operating system
  • IP address and approximate geolocation
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Search terms, referral sources, and clickstream data
This data allows us to understand how visitors use our website, improve site performance, and tailor our content and communication strategies accordingly.

2.3. Cookies and Tracking Tools
Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember your preferences and enhance your user experience. We use:
  • Essential Cookies – For website functionality
  • Performance Cookies – To gather website usage statistics
  • IP address and approximate geolocation
  • Marketing Cookies – To track user behavior and improve ad targeting
You can manage cookie settings through your browser or opt out through industry-standard platforms.
3. Why We Collect Your Data
We use your personal data to provide, support, and improve our services. Key purposes include:
  • Communication: Responding to your inquiries, processing your requests, and sending relevant updates.
  • Marketing (with consent): Delivering newsletters, promotional campaigns, and event invitations tailored to your preferences.
  • Website Experience: Understanding behavior to enhance design, speed, accessibility, and usability of our site.
  • Compliance: Meeting legal obligations, resolving disputes, and ensuring safe digital operations.
We never sell your personal data. Our aim is to create meaningful, secure, and beneficial interactions with everyone who engages with Avensol.
4. Legal Basis for Processing
For users within the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal grounds for processing personal data include:
  • Consent – You’ve granted us permission to use your information.
  • Contractual Obligation – When processing is necessary to fulfill our contract with you.
  • Legal Compliance – If required by law or court order.
  • Legitimate Interests – For business operations, marketing (where appropriate), and service improvements, provided your rights are not compromised.
5. How We Share Information
We may share your data with trusted partners and service providers under strict confidentiality terms. These include:
  • Email and CRM platforms (e.g., for newsletters)
  • Website hosting and analytics providers
  • Security and infrastructure services
  • Professional advisors (legal, financial, etc.)
  • Authorities or regulators when legally required
Every third-party partner is contractually obligated to maintain the security and confidentiality of your data.
6. Data Security Measures
Avensol uses advanced security protocols and technologies to ensure your information remains protected:
  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS
  • Secure access controls and user authentication
  • Regular vulnerability scans and software updates
  • Firewalls and malware prevention systems
  • Staff training on data protection and secure communication
We continuously monitor and improve our security infrastructure to protect against unauthorized access, breaches, and loss.
7. Data Retention
Your personal information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes it was collected. We consider:
  • The duration of your relationship with us
  • Any legal obligations requiring data retention
  • The need to resolve disputes or enforce agreements
When your data is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it in a secure manner.
8. Your Rights
You have the right to:
  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate or outdated data
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests or marketing
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly at [Insert Contact Email].
9. International Data Transfers
Our operations may require us to process or store your information outside of your country of residence. We ensure:
  • Transfers are compliant with applicable data protection laws
  • Adequate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place
  • Your data remains protected regardless of where it is processed
10. Third-Party Links
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect legal updates or operational changes. When updates occur, we’ll post the revised version with a new “Last Updated” date. We encourage users to review this policy regularly to stay informed.
